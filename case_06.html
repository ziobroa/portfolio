MICA Spring Concert

MICA Spring Concert was an event planned by myself and my partner, where our goal was to bring a Baltimore-based music scene to the MICA campus. One of my tasks with this event was designing its poster as well as marketing material.

Year

2016

Skills

Experience Design
Print Design


Process

I wanted the visual language to reflect the energy and personality of the artists and their music, which is Baltimore-based hip-hop. I used tactics such as color to draw immediate attention. I also used my digital collage head cut-outs to display important information; this was the focus of the event's identity.
