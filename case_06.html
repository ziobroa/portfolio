Zee's Bees

Nestled in the heart of New Jersey, Zee's Bees is about family and everything honey. Bottled up in nostalgic branding and embellished with friendly illustrations, Zee's Bees serves their local community with deliecious raw, unpasteurized honey.

Year

2022

Skills

Branding
Print Design


Process

The brand identity and logo for Zee's Bees is inspired by 70's typography and woodblock illustration. Since it is a small, family-owned business, Zee's Bees was designed to have the look and feel of something handmade, friendly, and approachable. The brand colors reflect the beautiful shades of gold that each harvest yields.
