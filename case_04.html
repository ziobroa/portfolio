PLASmu

PLASmu is an imaginary museum that is dedicated to the subject matter of plastic. The goal of this museum is to inform its viewers the history of plastic and its impact on the environment. In addition to this, this museum would showcase creative solutions and proposed/active campaigns to reduce plastic pollution.

Logo Development

The design of my logo for PLASmu is based on the chemical formula of plastic (or polyethylene). The process above shows how I took polyethylene backbones and simplified its composition into a box. This logo can mutate due to the different densities and different backbones of polyethylene; the visual growth from this square reminds me of the growing issue of plastic. Color Scheme

The brand's color palette for PLASmu is based off of the image research I collected through Google's browser engine on the topic of plastic pollution and general environmental concerns beyond the museum's primary scope. The three colors of the palette (besides off-white and off-black) come from three particular themes of environmental issues: air pollution, oil spillage, and plastic pollution.
